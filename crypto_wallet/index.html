Our Security

2FA Protected Accounts

Secure your funds with a 2FA Multisig Shield solution. One key is held on your device and another on our 2FA servers, enabling you to protect your wallet with two-factor authentication. Timelocks or a third backup key ensure you always retain full ownership of your funds.

Standard Singlesig Support

Easy to use and easy to install. You can spend your funds using your keys with any application that supports generally accepted wallet standards.

Confidentiality

No documents, personal information, or KYC required. An email address is needed for wallet recovery purposes only. Connect via Tor at the tap of a button.

Full API support

Bitkeeper is an easily customizable wallet that is accessible to developers and can be integrated with other applications if desired.
