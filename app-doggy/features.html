Features

Pet Monitor
Monitor your pet in real-time with our advanced LTE technology. This pet monitor allows you to keep an eye on your dog no matter where you are. The camera provides high-quality video streaming to ensure your pet’s safety and well-being. Stay connected to your furry friend with just a tap on your phone.

Notifications
Get instant notifications about your dog’s activities, including movement or noise detection. The system alerts you immediately, so you can take action if necessary. Stay informed about your dog’s behavior, ensuring their safety even when you’re away. Receive alerts on your phone for convenience and peace of mind.

Video Recording
Capture high-quality video recordings and screenshots to monitor your pet’s behavior over time. The dog surveillance camera offers continuous recording, ensuring you never miss a moment. Review video footage later to better understand your pet’s needs and activities. It’s an essential feature for detailed dog monitoring.

Voice Messages
Send voice messages to your dog through the built-in speaker. This dog monitor camera allows you to communicate with your pet, offering reassurance and comfort when you're not around, and helping maintain a connection even from a distance.

HD Quality
Enjoy crystal-clear video with HD quality, ensuring you can see every detail. The high-definition video feed allows you to keep a close watch on your pet’s activities, even in low light. This feature ensures that the dog monitor camera offers the best view of your pet at all times, day or night.

Night Mode
The night mode feature allows you to monitor your dog even in complete darkness. The camera’s infrared technology provides clear visibility at night, ensuring 24/7 surveillance. With this feature, your dog’s safety is guaranteed, whether it’s day or night.
The Doggy App is your all-in-one solution for pet monitoring, offering real-time video streaming, two-way audio, and motion alerts to keep you connected with your furry friends.
