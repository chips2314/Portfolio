frequently asked question

Can the camera be used if the devices are on different Wi-Fi networks?

Yes, the pet camera can be used even if the devices are on different Wi-Fi networks. The key is to ensure both devices are connected to the internet, allowing remote access to the camera. With a stable internet connection, you can monitor your pet from anywhere. Learn more about remote access.

What features ensure a pet's safety when the owner is not around?

The pet camera is designed with multiple safety features, including real-time video monitoring, motion detection, and two-way audio. These features help ensure your pet's safety and allow you to stay connected. The camera alerts you to any unusual activity, giving you peace of mind. Explore safety features here.

Is the camera suitable for night observation in a completely dark room?

Yes, many pet cameras, including our cat cam, are equipped with infrared night vision technology. This allows you to observe your pet clearly even in total darkness. With this feature, you'll never miss a moment of your pet's activity at night. Check out night vision capabilities.

Can you interact with your pet through the camera app?

Absolutely! The pet camera app includes two-way audio, allowing you to speak with your pet and hear their sounds. This interaction helps comfort them and strengthens your bond even when you are not physically present. Find out more about two-way communication.

How can the app help calm your pet remotely?

The app allows you to play soothing sounds or speak to your pet through the pet cam, which can help calm them down when they feel anxious. This feature is particularly useful when you're away and want to ensure your pet stays calm. Learn how to use calming features effectively.
