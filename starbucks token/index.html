What is STBUX?

What Makes STBUX Unique?

STBUX is a revolutionary deflationary token that gains value over time as its supply decreases. Each transaction contributes to the ecosystem through small taxes, a portion of which is burned, ensuring long-term scarcity. STBUX also powers our NFT Digital-Art Marketplace—a platform where coffee lovers and artists come together to create and trade unique digital assets. By supporting STBUX, you join a community committed to blending technology, art, and coffee culture.

  • Public ICO Sale

    • Real-time progress tracking on the website with milestones for funds raised.
  • Token Distribution
    • Distribute tokens to ICO participants after each stage via automated smart contract.
  • DEX Listing
    • List STBUX on decentralized exchanges (e.g., Raydium, Orca) immediately after ICO ends.
  • Expand Marketing Efforts
    • Begin partnerships with NFT marketplaces and Starbucks-related fan communities.
    • Launch educational campaigns about the deflationary token model and NFT platform.

Phase 3: Platform Development and Beta Testing (July – September 2025)

  • STBUX Wallet Beta Release
    • Launch the STBUX wallet app for storing and staking tokens.
    • Include features like Solana address integration and real-time token performance tracking.
  • First Token Burn Event
    • Burn 2% of the total supply from transaction fees to demonstrate the deflationary mechanism.
  • CEX Listing Phase 1
    • Apply and secure listings on mid-tier centralized exchanges (e.g., MEXC, Gate.io).

Phase 4: Full Launch and Expansion (October – December 2025)

  • STBUX Wallet Full Release
